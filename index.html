This is a dark iTerm theme with glowing colors.

baskerville made a nice .Xdefaults-to-iTerm2 script. More themes on his github.

To install:

  • Download or clone git, double click on 'hardcore.itermcolors'
  • Press CMD+i or CMD+, in iTerm2.app
  • Go to "Profiles" -> Tab "Colors"
  • Click on "Load Presets..." switch to "hardcore"
